Box Pleated

box-pleat curtains or tailored pleat curtains, are a popular style of window treatment characterized by neatly folded pleats that create a structured and sophisticated look.

Rod pocket

pocket curtains or casement curtains, are a type of window treatment that features a pocket or sleeve at the top of the curtain panel through which a curtain rod can be inserted.

Sheer Drapes

sheer curtains or voile curtains, are a type of window treatment made from lightweight, translucent fabric. They are designed to filter light, provide privacy, and add an airy and elegant look to a room.
